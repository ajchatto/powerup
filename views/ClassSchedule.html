The dance affair is a well-established dance school in San Jose. We have been offering dance classses in San Jose and Santa Clara for over 25 years. We feel dance training is an important and valuable, as well as enjoyable, step in a student’s life. We are excited and confident in our program and all it has to offer. We invite you to browse the site, visit our facility, try a class and join our dance family… Be a part of something good!

We offer the following classes:

Dance Classes for all ages through the school year

SUMMER Camps and Classes
Birthday Parties - Choose your theme!
Special Classes and Events all year round

Drop in Rates
60 min class $20, 75 min class $23, 90 min class $26, College Rate $13/hr class or $16/1.5 hr class (must show student ID)

Payment Policies:
All students pay for classes on a monthly basis. All tuition is due on the 1st of each month and is considered late if not received by the 5th of the month. Your tuition reserves your place in the class. Tuition rates are based on a 4-week average of classes per month. Some months have 5 weeks, some have 3 weeks. Our goal is to provide 38-40 weeks of instruction/rehearsal times Sept – June. You always pay the same amount per month September through June. You must give the studio a 30 day written notice if you are dropping a class. Otherwise, you will be continued to be billed. There are no credits or refunds for missed lessons and no partial month payments.

Annual Registration Fee:
$50 per family. Registration fees are non-refundable. An additional re-registration fee of $25 will be charged for those families who wish to take a “month off”. This includes the month of December.
